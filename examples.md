# ABCDE Examples (Prime Concept System)

How to say everything using only 8 atoms.

## Basic Phrases
- BA CE BE -> I like you
- BA CE CECA -> I like food
- E? BE ADA ACA -> Are you coming here?
- BA ADA ABE -> I am going there

## Daily Interaction
- A! CE -> Yes! / Good!
- A! DE -> No! / Bad!
- BABE CE BA -> Friend, I am happy
- BA DA CA -> I am making this

## Nature
- BA CE DEDE BADACA -> I like leaves
- CA DADA BADACA -> This is a big tree
- CEA CA DA BABA -> The sun warms us
- ACA CA -> Red thing (Fire / Blood)
- ADA CA -> Blue thing (Water / Sea)
- A CA -> White thing (Light / Snow)
- DEA CA -> Black thing (Night / Shadow)

## Tense & Natural Units
- E- BA DA CA -> I did it
- A- BA DA CA -> I will do it
- E? A- BA ADA ACA -> Will I come here? / Am I going to come here?
- A- BA ADA ACA E 3ACA -> I will come here after 3 days
- ACE ADA 1000BADA -> Paradise / a good place is 1000 steps away
- BA CE 5DEDECECA -> I want 5 eggs (or 5 egg-size units, by context)

## Names & External Concepts
- BACA [Yo4e] CE -> (You) Yo4e are good
- [Earth] CE BADACA -> Earth is a good plant (living world)
- BA DA [Programming] -> I am doing programming
- [Internet] E DA BABA -> The internet connects us

## Plurals & Intensity
- BACA BACA CE -> The people are good
- BA CE CECA CECA -> I want a lot of food (Feast)
- CA DADA DADA -> That is a giant thing

## Survival & Greetings (Subject Omission allowed)
- BABE CE -> こんにちは/さようなら（友達が良い）
- E? CECA CE -> [Context] Is the food good?
- A! CEDA BA -> [Urgent] Help me!
- CE BE -> [Greeting] Thank you (You are good)
- DE-DA CA -> [Command] Don't do that
